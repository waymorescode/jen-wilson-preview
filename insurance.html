Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take Medicare/Medicaid?

Please contact me directly to discuss Medicare/Medicaid coverage options. Insurance acceptance can vary, and I'm happy to help you understand your options.

What if my insurance isn't listed?

Contact Headway to verify coverage. Some plans may be accepted that aren't listed above. You can also use the "Check Insurance" button to see if your plan is covered.

Can I use my HSA/FSA?

Yes! Therapy is a qualified medical expense. You can use your Health Savings Account (HSA) or Flexible Spending Account (FSA) to pay for sessions.

Do you offer sliding scale fees?

Limited sliding scale slots are available for those experiencing financial hardship. Please inquire during your free consultation to discuss options.

How does insurance billing work?

Through Headway, insurance billing is simple. They verify your coverage, handle all claims, and you only pay your copay at each session. No paperwork or reimbursement hassles for you.

What if I want to use insurance but keep my therapy private?

I understand privacy concerns. When using insurance, a diagnosis is required for billing purposes and becomes part of your medical record. If you prefer complete privacy, you can choose to pay privately without using insurance.
