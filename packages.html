Wellness Packages

Intensive, focused work for specific life transitions

What These Are

Boutique wellness packages — structured, intensive programs designed around specific life transitions. These aren't group classes or generic programs. They're limited-enrollment, high-touch, built around your particular situation.

Each package is 4, 6, or 12 weeks of focused work with built-in support between sessions. You're invested, and I'm invested with you.

Available Packages

Emotional Wellness Transformation

For people ready to feel significantly better. We identify what's driving heaviness, build practices that work, develop resilience you can draw from.

Includes: 8-12 sessions, between-session support, personalized stress reduction.

Relationship Reboot

For couples ready to rebuild connection. We understand how you got here, rebuild that foundation of safety, navigate tensions differently.

Includes: 8-12 sessions together, individual sessions if helpful, practices for home.

The Good Mama Reset

For mothers reckoning with the gap between expectation and reality. Understand what you need, release the "good mother" image, build sustainable practices.

Midlife Reset

For people navigating profound shifts of midlife. Honor the loss and possibility both, move into the next chapter with clarity and intentionality.

Empty Nest Recalibration

For parents stepping into the next phase. Reclaim yourself, discover what you want, build a life that feels full again.

Pricing: Most packages range from $2,000–$5,000. Payment plans available.
