What therapy with me looks like, financially

Individual Therapy

Weekly sessions. Your pace. The work of understanding yourself more deeply and living more fully.

Couples Therapy

$300 per hour

The investment in your relationship—both of you, the space between you, and what's possible when you show up together.

Wellness Packages

Pricing is discussed during your consultation, scaled to the package length (4, 6, or 12 weeks) and the depth of between-session support. Payment plans are available.

Why Private Pay

I keep my practice private and limited because that's what allows genuine work. No insurance requirements dictating the shape of therapy. No volume pressure. Just you, the work that matters, and real time.

I provide superbills for out-of-network insurance reimbursement. You pay us directly; insurance reimburses you per your plan.

What You're Investing In

Not efficiency. Not billable hours. Real presence. Nearly twenty years of clinical depth. The kind of space where you can actually change.
