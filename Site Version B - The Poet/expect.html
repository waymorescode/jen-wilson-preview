What to Expect

How our work together unfolds

The Consultation Call

You reach out. We schedule a brief consultation—this is a real conversation, not a intake form. I want to hear what brought you, what you're hoping for, whether we're a good fit. You'll know things about how I work. And I'll have a sense of what you need. No pressure. No timeline. Just clarity.

Your First Session

When you come in, there's tea. There's a room that feels like it breathes—warm, natural light, the sound of the ocean if the windows are open. You'll notice the colors are soft. That matters. The whole environment is designed to help you relax enough to be honest.

We'll talk. Not a clinical questionnaire—a genuine conversation about what matters, what hurts, what you want to feel different. You'll do most of the talking. I'll listen in a way that probably feels different from how people usually listen. There's no rush. We'll figure out what comes next.

How Therapy Unfolds

Regular therapy is open-ended. We meet weekly (or the rhythm that fits your life), and the work is whatever emerges. Some weeks we're untangling patterns. Some weeks we're just sitting with something difficult. Some weeks we discover that what you thought was wrong with you is actually the part of you that's been trying to protect you.

There's no script. No predetermined endpoint. Just real work, at a pace that makes sense, in service of you feeling more like yourself.

The Structured Packages

If you want more—more intensivity, more support, a defined arc—we have packages. Designed for specific moments. Four weeks of focused engagement. Six weeks. Twelve. You'll have my attention, between-session check-ins, and a framework that holds you as you move through something.

What Makes This Different

I don't believe in therapy-by-the-book. I don't rush. I won't pathologize you. And I genuinely care whether you feel better. That care shows up as real questions, real presence, and real investment in your becoming whoever you're trying to be.
