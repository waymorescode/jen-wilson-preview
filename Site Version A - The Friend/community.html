Community

Connection, gatherings, and shared exploration

Why Community Matters

I believe real change doesn't happen in isolation. It happens in connection—first in the therapy room, but also with other people working on similar questions. That's why I host gatherings, workshops, and community events.

These aren't seminars. They're not upselling you on therapy. They're spaces where you can think about something important with other people who care about similar things.

What We're Planning

We host seasonal workshops and community gatherings—panels on relationships and identity, wellness conversations, space to think through the transitions that affect us all.

Stay tuned for upcoming events. For now, if you want to know when gatherings are scheduled, reach out and I'll add you to the community list.

Stay Connected

If you want to know about upcoming gatherings or stay loosely connected to this work, you can join our community list. No spam. Just invitations to events and conversations that feel relevant.

You can also reach out directly if you have ideas for gatherings or topics you'd like to explore together.
