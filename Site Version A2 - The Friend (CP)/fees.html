Fees

Transparent pricing for therapy and intensives

Individual Therapy

$200 per hour

Private pay. Superbills provided for out-of-network reimbursement if your insurance covers out-of-network therapy.

Most people meet weekly. Some meet bi-weekly. The frequency depends on what you need and what makes sense for your life.

Couples Therapy

$300 per hour

Private pay. Superbills provided.

We typically meet weekly for couples work. Sometimes people do a more intensive schedule in the beginning if there's something urgent to address.

Intensives

$1,800 (6 weeks) / $3,200 (12 weeks)

Structured, high-touch therapeutic work with between-session support. Explore the intensives to find the one shaped for your chapter.

Why Private Pay

Private pay options allow for enhanced confidentiality and discretion for your care, personalized, flexible sessions tailored to your needs and pace, and direct access with continuity under clear, transparent fees.

What you're investing in is real presence and nearly twenty years of clinical depth — a nurturing space where transformation happens. Fees support consistent, attentive care, skilled guidance, and the time needed for lasting change.

It also means I can work with you flexibly. If you need extra support between sessions, I can offer that. If we need to adjust our schedule, we can. If you need longer sessions sometimes, we can talk about it.

This model works well for people ready to invest in themselves and who want to work with someone who can really show up for their healing.

Questions About Payment

If you'd like to talk through options, I welcome that conversation. Some people do a mix of therapy and intensives. Some people start with an intensive and move into ongoing work. The point is to figure out what's actually sustainable for you.

Get in touch if you'd like to discuss options.
