Wellness Packages

Intensive, focused work

The Edits

These aren't your standard therapy packages. They're for people who know what they want to work on and are ready to actually commit to it — not just think about it, but do the work.

Each package is 4, 6, or 12 weeks. You get structured sessions (usually twice a week to start), plus between-session support and touchpoints. They're high-touch and limited enrollment, which means we're not overscheduled and you're getting real attention.

Enrollment is seasonal. That means we open it up, fill a small number of spots, and close it. That's intentional — it keeps the work focused and real.

Emotional Wellness Transformation

You're doing okay, but you're also done with "okay." Something needs to shift — maybe anxiety that shows up at work, or a sadness you can't quite explain, or just a flatness that you're tired of living with.

The Relationship Edit

You're with someone. And something's off. You're arguing about the same thing over and over, or you're just not connecting the way you used to. We're looking at the dynamic between you and figuring out what needs to happen.

The Good Mama Boost

You're a mom. Good job, you're doing it. But you're also running on fumes. This is about reclaiming yourself while you're still fully parenting. It's not about "better work-life balance." It's about understanding what you actually need.

Pricing discussed during consultation

Midlife Reset

Something's shifting. Maybe you're questioning choices you made ten years ago. Maybe you're wondering if there's more to your life than what you've built. Midlife isn't a crisis — it's an awakening.

The Empty Nest Discovery

Your kid left. And yes, you're happy for them. And also — who are you now? What do you do with all this space and time? This is about figuring out the next chapter. Not going backward. Forward.

4, 6, or 12 weeks
Pricing discussed during consultation

What's Included

  • Structured sessions (usually twice weekly) with clear outcomes
  • Between-session support — you can reach out if something comes up
  • Written reflections or takeaways from our work
  • A real sense that someone's invested in your progress
  • The space to actually do the work

Why Limited Enrollment

I'm not running a high-volume practice. When we do these packages, I'm committing to a small number of people for that block of time. That means I'm present, not squeezed. You feel that. The work's better because of it.
