Common Questions

What happens on the consultation call?

We'll talk about what brought you, what you're hoping for, and whether we're a good fit. We start with a real conversation — relaxed, open, focused on clarity.

Do you offer telehealth?

Yes, I offer secure video sessions for clients throughout Florida. Many find it convenient and equally effective.

What are your rates?

Individual therapy is $200/hour. Couples therapy is $300/hour. Wellness packages are priced based on length and support. See the Fees page for more details.

Do you accept insurance?

I keep my practice private. I provide superbills for out-of-network insurance reimbursement. You pay directly; insurance reimburses you per your plan.

What if I need to reschedule?

Life happens. Please provide notice when possible. We'll work it out.
