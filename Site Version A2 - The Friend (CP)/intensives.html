Intensives

Intensive, focused work

The Edits

These intensives are for people who know what they want to work on and are ready to commit to it — ready to do the meaningful, focused work that leads to real change.

Each intensive is 6 or 12 weeks. You get structured sessions (usually twice a week to start), plus between-session support and touchpoints. They are high-touch and limited enrollment, which means you receive genuine, focused attention throughout.

Enrollment is seasonal. We open a small number of spots, fill them, and close enrollment. This keeps the work focused, intentional, and deeply personal.

Emotional Wellness Transformation

You're ready for something to shift — maybe the anxiety that shows up at work, or a sadness you're carrying, or a flatness you're ready to move through. This intensive creates space for that transformation.

6 weeks: $1,800 | 12 weeks: $3,200

The Relationship Edit

You're with someone and you're ready to reconnect. Maybe you're caught in the same conversation loop, or the closeness has shifted. This intensive looks at the dynamic between you and builds a path forward together.

6 weeks: $1,800 | 12 weeks: $3,200

The Good Mama Boost

You're a mom, and you're doing it well. You're also ready to reclaim yourself while you're still fully parenting. This intensive is about understanding what you actually need and creating space for your own wellness alongside motherhood.

6 weeks: $1,800 | 12 weeks: $3,200

Midlife Reset

Something is shifting. Maybe you're revisiting choices you made ten years ago, or wondering what more your life holds. Midlife is an awakening — a chance to discover what matters most to you now.

6 weeks: $1,800 | 12 weeks: $3,200

The Empty Nest Discovery

Your kid left, and you're happy for them. You're also holding space and time that feels brand new. This intensive is about figuring out the next chapter — moving forward into what inspires you.

6 weeks: $1,800 | 12 weeks: $3,200

What's Included

  • Structured sessions (usually twice weekly) with clear outcomes
  • Between-session support — you can reach out if something comes up
  • Written reflections or takeaways from our work
  • A real sense that someone is invested in your progress
  • The space to actually do the work

Why Limited Enrollment

When I take on intensives, I commit to a small number of people for that block of time. That means I am fully present, fully available. You feel that. The work is better because of it.
