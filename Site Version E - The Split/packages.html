What's Included

Structured Sessions

Weekly sessions designed around your specific challenge. Each conversation builds on the last. Real momentum.

Between-Session Support

Access to me between sessions via email for clarification or support. We're doing this work together.

Written Reflections

Insights and takeaways after each session. Something to sit with between meetings.

Invested Presence

This isn't transactional. I'm genuinely invested in your transformation and available to you in a real way.

Why Limited Enrollment

When I commit to a package with you, I'm creating space for real depth and presence. That means I can only take on a certain number of intensive programs at a time. It's not about scarcity—it's about making sure everyone gets the focused attention they deserve.

These packages are seasonal, which also means you have natural beginning and ending points. Clear structure. A contained experience that actually creates transformation.
