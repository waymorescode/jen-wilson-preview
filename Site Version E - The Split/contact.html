Common Questions

What happens on the consultation call?

We'll talk for about 30 minutes. You'll tell me what's going on, what brought you to therapy, and what you're hoping for. I'll listen and ask questions to understand what matters most. No forms, no pressure, just real conversation. You'll know if we're a good fit, and I will too.

Do you offer telehealth?

Yes. I offer both in-person sessions at my office in St. Augustine Beach and virtual sessions via a secure HIPAA-compliant platform. We can switch between them based on your needs and schedule. Many people do in-person initially and then move to telehealth, or mix both.

What are your rates?

Individual therapy: $200/50-minute session | Couples therapy: $300/50-minute session | Intensive packages: Pricing varies based on structure. We discuss during the consultation.

Do you accept insurance?

I practice on a private pay basis. I do provide superbills after each session that you can submit to your insurance for out-of-network reimbursement, if your plan covers out-of-network mental health care. You can check your coverage or ask your insurance directly.

What if I need to reschedule?

Life happens. If you need to reschedule, let me know as soon as possible. I require 48 hours notice for cancellations to avoid the full session fee. We can usually find another time that works. The goal is making this sustainable and real for your actual life.
