A Warm Place to Begin

A complimentary fifteen-minute conversation — your first warm step toward care that meets you where you stand.

Your first conversation with me

Thank you for being here. This first conversation is yours — a gentle, unhurried meeting place where you can share what's bringing you in, hear how I work, and feel into whether we're aligned. Come exactly as you are, with whatever feels present for you today.

Some people arrive with a clear sense of what they're seeking. Others arrive with a feeling they're still learning to name. Both are welcome, and both are honored here.

What to Expect from the Call

01

What We'll Talk About

What's bringing you here. What you're hoping for. What warmth, healing, and wellness might look like in your life right now — shaped around your own words and your own pace.

02

How Long

Fifteen unhurried minutes. Time enough to feel each other, to ask what you'd like to know, and to land on a real sense of whether we're the right fit for each other.

03

What Happens Next

We land on clear next steps together — your first full session with me, a warm handoff toward a colleague who fits your needs, or a little more space to think it through. You leave with clarity.

The consultation call happens by phone or secure video — whichever feels most comfortable for you. Welcoming new clients from anywhere in Florida.

Your Invitation

Come exactly as you are — questions, hopes, whatever feels present. This conversation is your first warm step toward care that meets you where you stand.
