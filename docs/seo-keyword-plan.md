## Three new niche pages — recommended

Each one is a short, focused page targeting a service with high intent and low competition. These take a couple of hours each to write and produce and should rank quickly because almost nobody in St. Augustine targets them.

### 1. `/adoption-home-study/` — Adoption Home Study Services

- **Primary keyword:** `adoption home study Jacksonville Florida`
- **Why it wins:** Adoption home studies are required for nearly every adoption in Florida. Families search this exact phrase and call the first qualified social worker who answers. There are only a handful of LCSWs in NE Florida who do this work.
- **Length:** 400–600 words. What's involved, who needs one, your approach, fee, contact.

### 2. `/clinical-supervision/` — Clinical Supervision for Registered Interns

- **Primary keyword:** `LCSW clinical supervision Florida`
- **Secondary:** `registered intern supervisor Jacksonville`
- **Why it wins:** Florida requires registered interns to log supervision hours toward licensure. They search this term constantly and there are far fewer qualified supervisors than interns.
- **Length:** 400–600 words. Hours, format, fee, your supervision philosophy.

### 3. `/forensic-clinical-services/` — Forensic Clinical Evaluations

- **Primary keyword:** `forensic clinician St. Augustine`
- **Secondary:** `court-recognized clinical evaluation Florida`
- **Why it wins:** Attorneys and courts looking for qualified forensic clinicians find very few in NE Florida. This page positions you for that referral channel.
- **Sensitivity note:** This page should be quiet, professional, attorney-facing — not in the same warm voice as the rest of the site. We can collaborate on tone before publishing.

## Local SEO — the move that matters most

The keyword data is unanimous: the highest-volume, highest-intent searches in your category are proximity searches like *"therapist near me."* Winning those is about Google's local pack (the map results above the regular blue links), and that's about Google Business Profile, not website copy.

**Three things to confirm or fix:**

1. **Google Business Profile (GBP) is claimed and accurate.** This is in Phase 2 of the prior plan. We'll need ~15 minutes of your time to verify it under your Google Workspace login.
2. **NAP consistency.** Name, Address, Phone has to match *exactly* across your site, GBP, Psychology Today, and any directory. Inconsistencies dilute local ranking.
3. **`LocalBusiness` schema on the homepage.** Currently we have `MedicalBusiness` schema. We can extend it with `geo` coordinates, opening hours, and `hasMap` link. Quick edit.
