## Pre-setup decisions (need answers before starting)

### Decision 1: One Profile or Two?

This is the same question Craig asked. The answer depends on Jen's Jacksonville office:

- **One GBP** (St. Augustine Beach as primary, Jacksonville as service area): **Recommended if Jacksonville is virtual / flexible / borrowed-office / occasional.** Single brand presence, simpler to maintain.
- **Two GBPs** (separate listing for each location): **Required if both offices are real recurring physical locations** with distinct addresses, hours, and the same therapist physically present at each.

**Action:** ask Jen the Jacksonville question first. If recurring physical office → two profiles. If not → one profile with service-area expansion.

### Decision 2: Business Classification

Google asks this during setup and **the wrong answer can get the listing suspended.** Four options:

1. **Storefront business** — has a physical location customers visit during business hours (e.g., the St. Augustine Beach office)
2. **Service-area business with physical location** — has an office but ALSO serves customers at their location (e.g., a plumber's shop)
3. **Hybrid (storefront + service area)** — has a physical office AND serves a wider area remotely. **This is Jen.** She has a physical office (St. Augustine Beach) AND serves Florida-wide via telehealth.
4. **Unstaffed facility** — not applicable

**Action for Jen:** **Storefront with service area** — physical address at St. Augustine Beach, service area covering all of Florida (for telehealth) plus Jacksonville/St. Augustine specifically.

### Decision 3: Public Address — show or hide?

Therapist offices have a privacy convention here. Two options:

- **Show the office address publicly** — better for local-pack ranking, customers can map to it
- **Hide the address, show service area only** — Google allows this for service-area-only businesses; customers see the city/region but not the street

**Recommendation:** show the St. Augustine Beach office address publicly (real office, reception/lobby, no privacy issue). This maximizes local-pack visibility. Hide if Jen's office is in a shared building with other tenants who haven't consented to walk-ins, or if she has a real privacy concern.

## Setup steps

### Step 1 — Sign in to the right Google account

Go to **`business.google.com/add`** in an incognito window. Sign in with **Jen's Google Workspace email** (the one she uses for `@drjenniferwilson.com`).

> **Why this matters:** the GBP gets owned by whatever Google account creates it. Use a personal Gmail and you'll have to transfer ownership later. Use the Workspace account from day one.

### Step 2 — Search for the business name

Type *"Conscious Practice Therapy and Wellness"* or *"Dr. Jennifer Wilson"* — Google may have an unclaimed listing already (sometimes auto-generated from web mentions). If a match appears, **claim it instead of creating a new one.** Duplicates are a real problem and hard to merge later.

### Step 3 — Enter business information

- **Business name:** *Conscious Practice Therapy and Wellness — Dr. Jennifer Wilson, PhD, LCSW*
- **Primary category:** **Psychotherapist**
- **Secondary categories:** *Counselor*, *Mental Health Service*, *Family Counselor*, *Marriage Counselor* (add 3–5 — they expand the search queries you can match)
- **Address:** St. Augustine Beach office street address
- **Service area:** add St. Augustine, St. Augustine Beach, Jacksonville, Ponte Vedra Beach, Jacksonville Beach, Florida (state-wide for telehealth)
- **Phone:** +1 904-906-9403
- **Website:** `https://drjenniferwilson.com`
- **Hours:** Jen's actual operating hours

### Step 4 — Verification

Google decides your verification method automatically — you don't get to pick. In 2026, **video verification is the default** for most new therapist listings.

**If Google asks for video verification:**
- Record one continuous unedited take (no cuts, no offline uploads)
- Show: exterior signage, the office interior, your face, then a live action inside the GBP dashboard (e.g., editing a field while filming)
- Time: 2–5 minutes typically

**If Google offers postcard:** physical postcard with a 5-digit code mails to the address; arrives in 5–14 business days; enter the code in the dashboard to complete.

**If Google offers phone:** automated call or text with a code; instant.

**Tip:** If you start the verification flow on a phone (not desktop), Google is more likely to offer video since you have a camera ready.

## Post-verification — optimization (the parts that actually rank you)

Verification is just the start. The optimization is what wins the local pack.

### 1. Description (750 characters max)

Voice-checked. Lead with the primary keyword, end with a soft CTA.

**Draft:**
> *Dr. Jennifer Wilson, PhD, LCSW is a licensed clinical social worker and court-recognized forensic clinician offering boutique psychotherapy in St. Augustine Beach, Jacksonville, and across Florida by telehealth. With over twenty years of clinical experience, she works with women navigating midlife transitions, perimenopause, relationship strain, anxiety, parenting, caregiving, and trauma. Therapy is offered as weekly individual sessions, couples work, and structured 6- or 12-week intensives. Private pay; superbills provided for out-of-network reimbursement. Begin with a complimentary 15-minute consultation.*

(That's 706 characters — under the 750 limit.)

### 2. Services list

Add each as a distinct service with its own short description. These help Jen rank for service-specific searches:

- Individual Psychotherapy
- Couples Therapy
- Therapy Intensives (6 and 12 week)
- Perimenopause & Midlife Therapy
- Trauma Therapy
- Adoption Home Studies
- Clinical Supervision for Registered Interns
- Forensic Clinical Evaluations

Use the existing imagery in `studios/clients/jen-wilson/images/` — the headshot, beach photos, and interior shots are already approved.

### 4. Pre-seeded Q&A (Craig's tactic — worth doing)

Owners can post their own questions and answer them. These show in the listing. Pre-seed 6–8 to control the narrative:

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| Do you accept insurance? | *I'm a private-pay practice and don't bill insurance directly. I provide a superbill after each session that you can submit to your insurance for out-of-network reimbursement.* |
| What is a 15-minute consultation? | *A complimentary phone or video call to see if we're the right fit before scheduling a first session. No commitment — just a conversation.* |
| Do you offer telehealth? | *Yes — secure telehealth sessions are available to clients anywhere in Florida via Jane App.* |
| Where are your offices? | *I have offices in St. Augustine Beach and Jacksonville, with telehealth available across Florida.* |
| What ages do you work with? | *I work primarily with adults. Teen and family sessions are available in select cases — please ask during the consultation.* |
| What is a therapy intensive? | *A structured 6- or 12-week program focused on a specific life moment. More concentrated than weekly therapy, with high-touch support between sessions. Five named intensives are available — see drjenniferwilson.com/intensives.html.* |
| What are your fees? | *Individual sessions are $200, couples $300, and intensives are $1,800 (6 weeks) or $3,200 (12 weeks). Full details at drjenniferwilson.com/fees.html.* |
| How do I schedule? | *Visit drjenniferwilson.com/book to start with a complimentary 15-minute consultation.* |

### 5. Weekly posts (the maintenance work)

GBP "Posts" are 100–500-word updates that show in the listing. **Posting weekly is the single biggest ongoing-maintenance lever for staying ranked in the local pack.**

Post types and a starter rotation:

- Week 1: **What's New** — a brief practice update or new pillar page launch
- Week 2: **Offer / Event** — link to a current intensive enrolling
- Week 3: **Article highlight** — link to a blog post or pillar page
- Week 4: **Practice culture** — a thoughtful note in Jen's voice about how she works

This is the part Craig is selling at $250/mo Core Maintenance. If we're doing it ourselves, it's ~10 min/week.

### 6. Reviews

After verification, send a polite Google review request to past clients who've completed an intensive. **Five 5-star reviews changes the local pack ranking meaningfully.** Don't ask current clients (HIPAA-conscious — feels coercive). Past clients only, opt-in.

A review request template can live in `studios/clients/jen-wilson/site/docs/email-templates/` if you want me to draft one.
