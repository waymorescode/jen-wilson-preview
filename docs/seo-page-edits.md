## New pages — recommended additions

Each one is short (300–600 words), targets a unique keyword, and is straightforward to write.

### Tier 1 — niche service pages (highest leverage)

#### 1. `/adoption-home-study/` — Adoption Home Study Services

- **Primary keyword:** `adoption home study Jacksonville Florida`
- **Secondary:** `adoption home study St. Augustine`, `licensed home study evaluator Florida`
- **Title:** *Adoption Home Study — Dr. Jennifer Wilson, LCSW (Jacksonville & St. Augustine, FL)*
- **H1:** *Adoption Home Study Services in Jacksonville & St. Augustine*
- **Length:** 400–600 words
- **Sections:** What a home study involves · Who needs one · Your approach and timeline · Fee · How to begin
- **Why it wins:** Florida adoptions require this; few qualified LCSWs in NE Florida actively offer it; high-intent searchers ready to hire

#### 2. `/clinical-supervision/` — Clinical Supervision for Registered Interns

- **Primary keyword:** `LCSW clinical supervision Florida`
- **Secondary:** `registered intern supervisor Jacksonville`, `clinical supervisor St. Augustine`
- **Title:** *Clinical Supervision for Florida Registered Interns — Dr. Jennifer Wilson, LCSW*
- **H1:** *Clinical Supervision for Florida Registered Interns*
- **Length:** 400–600 words
- **Sections:** Who you supervise · Format and hours · Your supervision philosophy · Fee · How to begin
- **Why it wins:** Far fewer qualified supervisors than interns in Florida; predictable monthly retainer revenue

#### 3. `/forensic-clinical-services/` — Forensic Clinical Evaluations

- **Primary keyword:** `forensic clinician St. Augustine`
- **Secondary:** `court-recognized clinical evaluation Florida`, `forensic LCSW Jacksonville`
- **Title:** *Forensic Clinical Services — Dr. Jennifer Wilson, PhD, LCSW (St. Augustine, FL)*
- **H1:** *Forensic Clinical Services — St. Augustine & Jacksonville*
- **Length:** 400–600 words
- **Sections:** Scope of forensic work · Court qualifications · Areas of evaluation · Engagement process · Fee structure (consult-based)
- **Tone note:** Quieter, attorney-facing voice — different audience than the rest of the site. Draft together before publishing.
- **Why it wins:** Attorneys actively search for qualified forensic clinicians in NE Florida and find very few
