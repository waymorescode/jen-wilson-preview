# Conscious Practice Therapy — Messaging & Pitch Content

## Organizational Pitch

At Conscious Practice Therapy, we partner with healthcare organizations, schools, and community agencies to strengthen the quality, sustainability, and impact of mental health services.

Founded by Dr. Jennifer Wilson, LCSW, PhD, our work is grounded in trauma-informed, anti-oppressive, and relational approaches that honor both client and clinician well-being. We specialize in supporting professionals who are doing complex, high-stakes work—particularly in child and family systems.

We understand that today's mental health workforce is navigating increasing acuity, burnout, and systemic challenges. Our services are designed to meet that reality with depth, practicality, and compassion.

### What We Offer

**Clinical Supervision (Individual & Group)**
High-quality, reflective supervision that strengthens clinical skill, ethical decision-making, and professional identity.

**Workforce Development & Training**
Engaging, evidence-informed trainings on trauma, family systems, telehealth, cultural humility, and clinician resilience.

**Program Consultation**
Support for organizations looking to build or refine trauma-informed, equitable, and sustainable service delivery models.

**Clinician Wellness & Retention Support**
Practical strategies to reduce burnout, improve morale, and foster a culture of reflective, supported practice.

### Our Approach

We don't offer one-size-fits-all solutions. Our work is:

- **Relational** – We prioritize trust, safety, and authentic engagement
- **Practical** – Clinicians leave with tools they can use immediately
- **Reflective** – We create space for meaning-making, not just productivity
- **Equity-Centered** – We intentionally address power, identity, and systemic context

### Why Partner With Us

Organizations choose Conscious Practice Therapy because we bridge the gap between theory and real-world practice. We understand the pressures your teams face—and we provide support that is both clinically rigorous and deeply human.
