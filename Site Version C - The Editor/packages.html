Wellness Packages

Structured, intensive work for specific moments in your life

What These Are

These are structured, intensive programs for people ready to commit to meaningful change in a focused area over 4, 6, or 12 weeks. They're high-touch. Enrollment is limited so that everyone gets real attention. Each package includes weekly sessions, between-session support, and clear outcomes.

Think of them as a different kind of investment—shorter than ongoing therapy, more intentional, with a specific goal in mind.

Emotional Wellness Transformation

4, 6, or 12 weeks

For people who want to shift how they're feeling in their daily life. Better mood. More resilience. Less anxiety or overwhelm. A restored sense of agency.

What's included: Weekly sessions tailored to your specific emotional landscape. Check-ins between sessions. Tools you can use when things get hard. By the end, you'll understand your patterns better and have practical ways to shift them.

Relationship Reboot

6 or 12 weeks

For couples who want to strengthen their connection, work through recurring conflict, or rebuild trust. This isn't about fixing—it's about moving to a healthier place.

What's included: Weekly sessions together. Guided conversations you can practice at home. Practical tools for navigating conflict. By the end, you'll have better tools for staying connected and clearer sense of what you both want.

The Good Mama Reset

6 or 12 weeks

For mothers navigating looking fine while feeling overwhelmed. This work is about reclaiming yourself while building a sustainable relationship with motherhood.

What's included: Weekly sessions focused on your experience as a mother. Tools for boundary-setting and reconnecting with yourself. Space to process what this phase is actually like. By the end, you'll feel less alone and more capable.

Midlife Reset

6 or 12 weeks

For people in the middle of their lives noticing the road ahead doesn't look as imagined. This work is about getting clarity and moving forward with intention.

What's included: Weekly sessions exploring what's being asked of you right now. Clarity work around what matters at this stage. Conversations about what you want the next chapter to hold. By the end, you'll have clearer sense of direction.

Empty Nest Recalibration

6 or 12 weeks

For parents rebuilding an identity that isn't primarily about being a parent. Who are you now? What do you actually want? This work helps you move through this transition.

What's included: Weekly sessions focused on this life transition. Exploration of identity beyond parenting. Conversations about relationships, purpose, and what comes next. By the end, you'll have moved through the disorientation and started building something new.

How Enrollment Works

Enrollment opens seasonally—typically in spring, summer, and fall. Spots are limited. If you're interested, book a consultation and we'll talk about timing, which package makes sense, and whether the next cohort is a good fit.

Pricing for packages is discussed during the consultation.
