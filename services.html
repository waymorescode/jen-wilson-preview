Therapy Services

Compassionate, Evidence-Based Support for Your Healing Journey

I offer specialized therapy services designed to help you heal, grow, and thrive. Each person's journey is unique, and I tailor my approach to meet your specific needs and goals.

Individual Therapy

Trauma & PTSD Recovery

Living with trauma feels like carrying an invisible weight that affects every part of your life. You don't have to carry it alone anymore.

I specialize in helping people heal from:

  • Childhood trauma and abuse
  • Sexual trauma and assault
  • Accidents and medical trauma
  • Emotional and physical abuse
  • Complex PTSD from repeated trauma

Using EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) and trauma-informed therapy, we'll work at your pace to process these experiences safely. Many clients experience significant relief within 3-6 months.

Learn More About Trauma Therapy →

Anxiety & Depression Treatment

When anxiety keeps you up at night or depression makes everything feel impossible, therapy can help you find your way back to yourself.

I help with:

  • Generalized anxiety and panic attacks
  • Social anxiety
  • Perfectionism and imposter syndrome
  • Depression and hopelessness
  • Postpartum anxiety and depression
  • Burnout and overwhelm

Together, we'll develop practical strategies to manage symptoms while addressing the root causes of your distress.

Learn More About Anxiety & Depression Treatment →

Eating Disorders & Body Image

Your relationship with food and your body doesn't have to be a constant battle. Recovery is possible, and you deserve support.

I provide specialized treatment for:

  • Anorexia nervosa
  • Bulimia nervosa
  • Binge eating disorder
  • Emotional eating
  • Body dysmorphia
  • Exercise addiction

My approach addresses the pain underneath the behaviors, helping you heal your relationship with food, your body, and yourself.

Learn More About Eating Disorder Treatment →

Couples & Family Therapy

Couples Therapy

Every relationship faces challenges. Whether you're dealing with communication breakdowns, trust issues, or major life transitions, couples therapy provides a safe space to reconnect and rebuild.

I help couples with:

  • Improving communication
  • Rebuilding trust after betrayal
  • Navigating major life changes
  • Increasing intimacy and connection
  • Resolving recurring conflicts
  • Deciding about the future of the relationship
Learn More About Couples Therapy →

Family Therapy

Family relationships can be our greatest source of support—and sometimes our greatest challenge. Family therapy helps create healthier patterns that benefit everyone.

I work with families facing:

  • Parent-child conflict
  • Behavioral concerns in children/teens
  • Blended family challenges
  • Communication breakdowns
  • Major transitions (divorce, moves, loss)
  • Trauma affecting the whole family
