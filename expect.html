What to Expect

How therapy unfolds

Starting Out

When you reach out, you'll schedule a consultation call. This is a chance to talk about what's happening and what you hope might shift. I'll listen carefully and be honest about whether I'm the right fit.

Most consultations are 20 minutes. You'll get a sense of how I listen, and I'll get a sense of what we'd be working with.

The First Session

If we both agree to work together, your first full session is typically 60 minutes. We'll go deeper into your history, relationships, and what brought you here. This isn't random — I'm building a picture of how you developed and what makes sense about how you respond.

How Ongoing Therapy Works

Sessions are typically 50 minutes, once a week. Some do twice a week early on. We work at the pace that matches what you need.

What happens in sessions is a mix. We talk about what's happening now. We explore patterns that go back further. Sometimes I challenge gently. Sometimes I reflect. Sometimes we're quiet together.

The work is collaborative. I'm not the expert on your life — you are. I'm the expert on helping you become clearer.

What's Different About My Approach

I don't work from a protocol. I'm attuned to your interior world. I work somatically — understanding that what you feel in your body is information. Healing happens in the body too, not just the mind.

I bring mindfulness into our work — the ability to notice your own experience without judgment, to stay present to what's actually happening.

The Pace

Therapy isn't linear. There are weeks where something major shifts. There are weeks where you feel like you're going in circles. Both are part of it.

Some people work with me for a few focused months around a transition. Some for years, deepening self-understanding. There's no prescription. We adjust based on what's actually helping.
