What to Expect

From consultation to ongoing work

The Consultation Call

A real conversation, not an intake form. I want to hear what brought you, what you're hoping for, whether we're a good fit. No pressure. Just clarity.

Your First Session

Tea. A room that feels like it breathes. We'll talk—genuine conversation about what matters. You'll do most of the talking. I'll listen in a way that probably feels different. There's no rush.

How Therapy Unfolds

Regular therapy is open-ended. We meet weekly, and the work is whatever emerges. Some weeks we're untangling patterns. Some weeks we're sitting with something difficult. Just real work, at a pace that makes sense.

The Structured Packages

If you want more—more intensivity, more support, a defined arc—we have packages. 4, 6, or 12 weeks of structured work with between-session support.

What Makes This Different

I don't believe in therapy-by-the-book. I don't rush. I won't pathologize you. I genuinely care whether you feel better. That care shows up as real questions, real presence, and real investment in your becoming whoever you're trying to be.
