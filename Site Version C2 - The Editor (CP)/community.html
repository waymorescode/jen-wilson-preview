Community

Connection, learning, and shared experience

More Than a Practice

I'm rooted here. My family is here. I'm part of this community — not someone who rents office space and disappears.

Beyond one-on-one work, I'm committed to building connection and offering support to the broader community. That might look like workshops, panels, community gatherings — spaces where people can show up, learn, and connect around shared experiences.

These aren't seminars designed to funnel you into therapy (though if therapy feels right for you after, I'm here). They're genuine offerings, rooted in the belief that what we're all working with — how to be ourselves, how to stay connected, how to navigate change — is best worked on together sometimes.

Events & Gatherings

I host workshops and community conversations around themes like navigating major life transitions, rebuilding connection in long-term relationships, midlife reckoning, parenting with more ease, and what happens when you finally stop running.
