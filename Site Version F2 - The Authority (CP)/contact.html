What to Expect

The first step is a consultation call — a real conversation about whether we're aligned. No intake forms. No pressure. Just clarity.

Questions About Getting Started

What happens on the consultation call?

We'll talk about what brought you, what you're hoping for, and whether we're a good fit. It's a real conversation, not an intake form. No pressure. Just clarity.

How long are sessions?

Individual sessions are 50 minutes. Couples sessions are 75 minutes. We can talk about what makes sense for you.

Do you take insurance?

I'm private pay. I can provide a superbill for you to submit to your insurance for potential out-of-network reimbursement.

What if I'm not sure therapy is right for me?

That's okay. The consultation call is a good way to figure that out. No commitment. Just a conversation.
