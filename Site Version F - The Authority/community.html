Community

Building connection, fostering growth

Why Community Matters

Healing and growth don't happen in isolation. They happen in spaces where people feel seen, where vulnerability is safe, where we recognize that the struggles we thought were ours alone are shared.

My practice isn't just about individual or couples therapy. It's about fostering spaces—whether large or small—where accomplished, thoughtful people can show up authentically and connect around what matters.

Community is where we remember that we're not meant to figure it all out alone. It's where we learn from each other. Where we build belonging.

What We're Planning

I'm building community initiatives rooted in genuine connection, not marketing. These are invitations to real conversation—with me, and with each other. In the works:

Conversation Series

Intimate gatherings in St. Augustine Beach and Jacksonville exploring themes that matter: identity and purpose, midlife transitions, parenting with presence, the courage to ask for help.

Small groups. Real conversations. No agendas beyond authentic connection.

Workshops & Learning

For those interested in deeper work around specific topics—communication skills, anxiety management, navigating life transitions, building resilience—I'm developing small-group workshops.

These are designed for people who want to learn and practice new skills in a supportive community.

Newsletter & Reflections

Periodic thoughts on therapy, psychology, living well, what I'm noticing in my practice and in the world. Not marketing—just reflections that might matter to people thinking about these things.

For those who want to stay connected without committing to an event.
