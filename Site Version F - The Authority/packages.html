Packages

Intensive, focused work for specific life moments

These Aren't Standard Therapy Packages

They're for people who know what they want to work on and are ready to actually commit to it — not just think about it, but do the work.

Each package is 4, 6, or 12 weeks. You get structured sessions (usually twice a week to start), plus between-session support and touchpoints. They're high-touch and limited enrollment, which means we're not overscheduled and you're getting real attention.

Enrollment is seasonal. That means we open it up, fill a small number of spots, and close it. That's intentional — it keeps the work focused and real.

What's Included in Every Package

Intensive Sessions

Weekly or twice-weekly sessions, depending on the package. These aren't rushed—they're designed for depth and momentum.

Personalized Approach

We build a roadmap together. The package is shaped around your specific goals and timeline, not a one-size-fits-all model.

Support Between Sessions

Email access for pressing matters between sessions. This matters—the work continues in your life, not just in the room.

Tools and Practices

You learn practical, evidence-based tools—mindfulness, somatic practices, communication skills—that you can use long after the package ends.

Integration Sessions

The final 1-2 sessions focus on consolidating what you've learned and planning how to sustain this work after the package concludes.

Limited Enrollment

I keep my caseload small and accept a limited number of package clients at a time. This ensures you get my best, most focused attention.

Why Limited Enrollment?

I'm not running a high-volume practice. When we do these packages, I'm committing to a small number of people for that block of time. That means I'm present, not squeezed. You feel that. The work's better because of it.
