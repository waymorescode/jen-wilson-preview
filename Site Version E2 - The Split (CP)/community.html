Community

Connection, gatherings, and shared exploration

Why Community Matters

One of the most healing experiences is realizing you're not alone in what you're feeling. That other accomplished, put-together people are also wondering if there's more. That the struggles you thought were unique to you are universal human experiences.

Therapy is individual and private. And we also believe in the power of connection—gathering in person, talking about real things, learning from each other's perspectives and experiences.

We're creating spaces where that happens. Seasonal gatherings in Jacksonville and St. Augustine Beach. Conversations that matter. A community of people committed to feeling better and living more authentically.

What We're Planning

Seasonal Workshops

Focused learning experiences on topics that matter: navigating transition, strengthening relationships, deepening self-awareness, building resilience. Expert-led. Small groups. Real substance.

Panels & Conversations

Bringing together other professionals, researchers, and thought leaders to explore big questions: How do high-achieving women navigate authenticity? What does healthy aging look like? How do we parent with intention?

Wellness Conversations

Drop-in gatherings—sometimes in person, sometimes online—where we talk about what's real: anxiety, perfectionism, finding meaning, the pressure to have it all figured out. Just honest conversation in good company.

Community Events

Intentional gatherings in St. Augustine Beach and Jacksonville—morning walks, coffee conversations, seasonal celebrations. Building real connection with people who get it.

Stay Connected

Follow along for community events, workshops, and invitations. We share updates about what's coming, how to join, and why we're doing this work together.

Want to be in the loop? Drop us a note at hello@consciouspractice.com and we'll add you to our community list.

No spam. No pressure. Just authentic updates about what we're creating together.
