Why Private Pay

No Insurance Limits

Insurance dictates how many sessions you get and what you can work on. With private pay, we decide together what your therapy needs to look like—not an insurance company.

Flexibility

You control the pace, frequency, and duration of our work. If you need to pause, shift to telehealth, or intensify, we just make it work together.

High-Touch Care

I can respond to you via email between sessions. I can adjust our work on a week-by-week basis. I'm genuinely available in a way that insurance-based practice doesn't allow.

Privacy

No insurance claims. No diagnosis on a record that might follow you. Just you, me, and our work together.

Questions About Payment

How do I pay?

We can arrange payment via check, transfer, or credit card, whatever works best for you. We'll discuss logistics during our consultation call.

Do you offer payment plans?

We can discuss options. Some people do weekly payment, others monthly. Let's find what actually works for your budget and life.

What about superbills for insurance?

I provide superbills after each session that you can submit to your insurance for out-of-network reimbursement. It's your choice whether to do that.

What if cost is a real barrier?

Let's talk about it during your consultation. I don't want cost to prevent you from getting care. We might explore different options or frequency that's more sustainable for you.
