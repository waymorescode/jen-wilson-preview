Privacy Policy

How We Protect Your Information

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Your privacy and confidentiality are foundational to the therapeutic relationship. I am committed to protecting your personal health information and maintaining the highest standards of confidentiality. My privacy practices comply with HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act) and Florida state law.

Information We Collect

During our therapeutic relationship, I collect information to provide you with appropriate care, including: your name, contact information, insurance details (if applicable), medical history, mental health history, current medications, emergency contacts, and the content of our sessions.

I also collect information about website visitors (such as IP addresses and pages visited) through standard web analytics, which helps improve our site. This information is not linked to personally identifiable information.

How We Use Your Information

Your health information is used for treatment (providing therapy), payment (billing and payment processing), and healthcare operations (scheduling, administrative communications). I do not use your information for marketing or promotional purposes without your explicit consent.

Information shared during therapy sessions is documented in clinical records and used only for the purpose of providing you with quality mental health care.

Disclosure Situations

Your health information is confidential and will not be disclosed without your written authorization, except in the following circumstances:

  • To report abuse or neglect of a child or vulnerable adult
  • If you present a danger to yourself or others
  • Pursuant to a valid court order or subpoena
  • When required by law or for public health purposes
  • To consult with other healthcare providers about your care (only with your consent)
  • If you are in therapy as a condition of the legal system

Your Patient Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your medical records
  • Request amendments to your records
  • Receive an accounting of disclosures of your information
  • Request restrictions on uses and disclosures (though I am not required to agree to all requests)
  • Choose how you receive communications about your health
  • Withdraw authorization for disclosures

Website Privacy

This website uses standard web analytics to help me understand how visitors use the site. This information does not identify you personally and is used only to improve the website experience. We do not intentionally collect personal information through the website except when you choose to provide it (such as through a contact form).

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your computer. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Our use of cookies is limited and does not track personally identifiable information.

Security Measures

I implement comprehensive security measures to protect your health information from unauthorized access, including: encrypted electronic health records, secure password protection, physical safeguards for paper records, and limited access to information on a need-to-know basis.

While I take extensive precautions to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission is completely secure. By participating in therapy, you accept that some level of risk exists.

Telehealth Privacy

Telehealth sessions are conducted through a HIPAA-compliant secure platform. By participating in telehealth, you agree to connect from a private location where your session cannot be observed or overheard. I cannot guarantee the security of connections made from public networks or shared devices. You are responsible for maintaining the privacy of your location during telehealth sessions.

Third-Party Services

I use third-party services for scheduling (SimplePractice), telehealth (secure HIPAA-compliant platform), and payment processing. These services are contractually required to maintain the confidentiality of your information. I do not share your information with any third party without your consent, except as necessary for the operation of these services.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued participation in therapy constitutes your acceptance of any changes. If you have concerns about changes, please contact me to discuss.

Questions About Privacy

If you have questions about how your information is used or protected, or if you wish to exercise any of your patient rights, please contact me.
