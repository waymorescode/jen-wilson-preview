Professional Services

Clinical Supervision & Nonprofit Consultation

In addition to my therapy practice, I provide clinical supervision for therapists pursuing licensure and consultation to nonprofit organizations serving vulnerable populations. This work allows me to support the broader mental health community while maintaining my primary focus on individual and family therapy.

Clinical Supervision for Therapists

I provide clinical supervision for graduate students and pre-licensed therapists working toward their LCSW or LMFT licensure in Florida.

What I Offer

  • Individual and group supervision
  • Trauma-informed care training and consultation
  • Case conceptualization and treatment planning
  • EMDR consultation for therapists in training
  • Ethical decision-making guidance
  • Support for difficult cases and challenging client situations
  • Professional development and career guidance

My Supervision Approach

I believe supervision should be supportive, not punitive. My goal is to help you develop clinical skills, confidence, and your own therapeutic style while ensuring client safety and ethical practice. I draw on 20+ years of clinical experience and my background in trauma treatment, eating disorders, and family therapy.

Qualifications

  • PhD in Social Work
  • Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW #8431)
  • 20+ years of clinical experience
  • Former Director of Clinical Services
  • University faculty experience at three institutions
  • Approved clinical supervisor in Florida

Nonprofit Consultation

I provide consultation services to nonprofit organizations serving vulnerable populations, particularly those working with trauma survivors, families in crisis, and individuals experiencing mental health challenges.

Services Offered

  • Program development and evaluation
  • Trauma-informed organizational practices
  • Staff training on trauma, mental health, and crisis intervention
  • Clinical consultation for complex cases
  • Policy development and review
  • Grant writing and program design consultation
  • Outcome measurement and data analysis

Experience

As former Director of Clinical Services at Vita Nova (2010-2014), I have extensive experience in nonprofit leadership, program development, and working with vulnerable populations. I've also served as a consultant to multiple organizations serving trauma survivors and families in crisis.

Who I Work With

  • Domestic violence and sexual assault programs
  • Child welfare and family services organizations
  • Mental health and substance abuse treatment programs
  • Community health centers
  • Youth and family support organizations

Training & Workshops

I provide training and workshops for mental health professionals, nonprofit staff, and community organizations on topics including:

  • Trauma-Informed Care: Understanding trauma, its impact, and how to create trauma-informed practices
  • EMDR Basics: Introduction to EMDR therapy for trauma treatment
  • Eating Disorders: Recognition, assessment, and treatment approaches
  • Working with Complex Trauma: Treating clients with histories of chronic abuse and neglect
  • Self-Care for Helpers: Preventing burnout and compassion fatigue
  • Crisis Intervention: Responding to mental health crises and suicidal ideation

Trainings can be customized to your organization's needs and delivered in-person or virtually.

Academic & Research Background

My professional services are informed by both extensive clinical experience and academic research:

  • PhD in Social Work from Florida International University
