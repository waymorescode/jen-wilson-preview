Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer telehealth?

Yes! I offer secure video sessions for clients throughout Florida. Many clients find telehealth convenient and just as effective as in-person therapy.

What if I need to cancel?

I understand that life happens. Please provide 48 hours notice when possible. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

How long does therapy take?

Everyone's journey is different. Some concerns improve in 8-12 sessions, while deeper trauma work may take longer. We'll discuss your goals and timeline in our first session.

Do you prescribe medication?

As a PhD and LCSW, I don't prescribe medication. However, I can coordinate with your doctor or provide referrals to psychiatrists if medication might be helpful.
