What to Expect

The first step is a consultation call — a real conversation about whether we're aligned. A relaxed, welcoming space focused on clarity and connection.

Questions About Getting Started

What happens on the consultation call?

We'll talk about what brought you, what you're hoping for, and whether we're a good fit. A real conversation in a relaxed, welcoming space — focused on clarity and hope.

How long are sessions?

Individual sessions are 50 minutes. Couples sessions are 60 minutes. We can talk about what makes sense for you.

How does payment work?

All sessions are private pay, which allows for enhanced confidentiality and personalized, flexible care tailored to your needs. A superbill is available for you to submit to your insurance for potential out-of-network reimbursement.

I'm still exploring whether therapy is the right step.

Exploring is a beautiful place to start. The consultation call is a warm, open conversation designed to help you find clarity and see what feels right for you.

If you've read this far, something brought you here. Trust that instinct. Change is possible, and you deserve compassionate support along the way. Don't worry if you don't know where to start. Just schedule the call.
