Terms of Service

Client Policies & Agreements

Last Updated: October 2025

These Terms of Service outline the policies and agreements for therapy services provided by Dr. Jennifer Wilson, PhD, LCSW, at Dr. Jennifer Wilson. By scheduling an appointment, you agree to these terms.

Professional Relationship

The therapeutic relationship is a professional relationship designed to help you achieve your mental health goals. It is not a social or business relationship. Dr. Wilson cannot have dual relationships with clients (e.g., cannot be your therapist and your friend, business partner, or have any other relationship outside of therapy).

Appointments & Scheduling

Session Length

Standard therapy sessions are 50 minutes. Extended sessions (80 minutes) are available upon request.

Scheduling

Appointments can be scheduled via phone, email, or our online booking system. We will make every effort to accommodate your preferred times, though availability may be limited.

Late Arrivals

If you arrive late to your appointment, the session will end at the originally scheduled time. You will be charged for the full session.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that emergencies and unexpected situations arise. However, to respect everyone's time and maintain availability for clients in need:

  • Please provide at least 48 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule
  • Cancellations with less than 48 hours notice will be charged the full session fee
  • No-shows (failure to attend without notice) will be charged the full session fee
  • Insurance does not cover late cancellations or no-shows

Exceptions may be made for true emergencies (serious illness, hospitalization, family emergency). Please contact us as soon as possible in these situations.

Fees & Payment

Session Fees

Current fees are listed on our Insurance & Rates page. Fees are subject to change with 30 days notice.

Payment

Payment is due at the time of service. We accept credit/debit cards, HSA/FSA cards, and checks. For insurance clients, copays are due at each session.

Insurance

We accept most major insurance plans through Headway. You are responsible for understanding your insurance benefits, including copays, deductibles, and coverage limits. If your insurance denies a claim, you are responsible for the full fee.

Outstanding Balances

Unpaid balances over 30 days may result in suspension of services until the balance is paid. Accounts over 90 days past due may be sent to collections.

Confidentiality

Your therapy sessions and records are confidential, as outlined in our Privacy Policy. However, there are legal exceptions where I am required to break confidentiality:

  • If you pose a serious danger of harm to yourself or others
  • If there is reasonable suspicion of child, elder, or dependent adult abuse or neglect
  • If a court orders the release of records
  • If you provide written authorization to release information

Communication Between Sessions

Email & Phone

Email and phone are for scheduling and brief administrative matters only. Please do not use email for therapeutic content or crisis situations. Email is not a secure form of communication.

Response Time

I typically respond to non-urgent messages within 24-48 business hours (Monday-Friday). I do not provide crisis intervention via phone or email.

Crisis Situations

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please:

  • Call 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)
  • Go to your nearest emergency room
  • Call 911

Telehealth Services

Telehealth sessions are conducted via HIPAA-compliant video platforms. By participating in telehealth, you acknowledge:

  • You are physically located in Florida during sessions (required by law)
  • You are in a private, confidential location
  • You understand the risks and limitations of telehealth
  • You have a backup plan if technology fails (phone number to call)
  • You have emergency contact information readily available

Termination of Services

Client-Initiated Termination

You have the right to terminate therapy at any time. I recommend a termination session to process your experience and discuss next steps, but this is not required.

Therapist-Initiated Termination

I may terminate therapy if:

  • You are not benefiting from therapy or need a different level of care
  • There is a conflict of interest or dual relationship
  • You have not paid your bill despite reasonable attempts to collect
  • You have missed multiple appointments without notice
  • You engage in threatening or inappropriate behavior

If I terminate services, I will provide appropriate referrals and offer a termination session when appropriate.

Records & Documentation

I maintain clinical records as required by law and professional standards. You have the right to request copies of your records (fees may apply). Records are retained for 7 years after termination of services, as required by Florida law.

Social Media & Online Interaction

To maintain appropriate professional boundaries and protect your confidentiality:

  • I do not accept friend or connection requests from current or former clients on social media
  • I do not interact with clients on social media
  • I do not search for clients on social media
  • If you choose to follow or interact with my professional pages, please be aware this may compromise your confidentiality

Limitations of Services

Please understand that:

  • Therapy is not a substitute for medical care or emergency services
  • I cannot guarantee specific outcomes or results
  • Therapy requires active participation and may be challenging at times
  • Progress depends on many factors, including your commitment to the process
  • I do not provide court testimony, custody evaluations, or forensic services

Consent to Treatment

By scheduling an appointment and attending therapy sessions, you consent to treatment and acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to these Terms of Service. You will be asked to sign a formal consent form at your first appointment.
