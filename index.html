Welcome. I'm Dr. Jennifer Wilson.

If you're lying awake at 2 AM with racing thoughts, struggling to keep it all together while anxiety eats away at you, or carrying the weight of past trauma that still affects your daily life—you've found the right place.

I specialize in helping women and families in St. Augustine heal from anxiety, depression, eating disorders, and trauma. With over 20 years of experience and advanced training in EMDR and trauma-informed care, I provide a safe space where healing becomes possible.

Whether you're a mother juggling endless responsibilities, a professional woman battling perfectionism, or someone working to heal from past wounds, I'm here to support your journey toward peace and wholeness.
