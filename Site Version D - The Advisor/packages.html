Wellness Packages

Structured pathways for life's turning points

The Idea Behind Them

Life has turning points. Moments when you want more than weekly conversation—you want immersion, clarity, someone in your corner as you move through something real. Our packages are built for those moments: high-touch, limited enrollment, shaped for whatever chapter you're navigating.

Each one is its own arc: intention, engagement, integration. Four weeks. Six weeks. Twelve weeks of structured work with between-session support, so the momentum builds.

Emotional Wellness Transformation

For the moment you're ready to feel more fully

You're managing. Appearances are fine. But somewhere in the quiet, you want to feel more alive, more present, more like yourself. Maybe anxiety is stealing hours. Maybe you're numb and can't remember when that started. Maybe you just know something needs to change.

This is four weeks of focused emotional recalibration. We meet weekly, and in between, you'll have direct support as you notice what shifts. The goal: move from managing to actually flourishing.

Investment: Discussed during consultation

Relationship Reboot

For couples ready to reconnect

The distance between you feels permanent. Or new. Or maybe you can't remember the last time you felt like partners instead of logistics managers. This package is six weeks of actual repair: deeper conversations, genuine understanding of each other, real tools for reconnecting.

You'll both be heard. Both challenged. Both reminded why this matters.

Investment: Discussed during consultation

The Good Mama Reset

For mothers reconsidering everything

Motherhood is radiant and also often invisible. You're holding so much—the logistics, the anxiety, the weight of doing it right. What would it feel like to put yourself back in the center of your own life? This six-week package is for mothers ready to reclaim something for themselves: presence, clarity, groundedness, or just remembering who you are underneath the role.

Investment: Discussed during consultation

Midlife Reset

For the transition nobody acknowledges

Your kids are growing. Your career is clear (or unclear). Your body is changing. Your priorities are shifting. This isn't crisis—it's awakening. And you want support moving through it consciously instead of drifting. Twelve weeks to sit with what's real, what you want, and who you're becoming.

Investment: Discussed during consultation

Empty Nest Recalibration

For the horizon suddenly appearing

Your identity was so intertwined with active parenting that now, in the quiet house, you're wondering who you are. This is real. It's also an invitation. Twelve weeks to rediscover yourself, re-engage with your partner, and step into this next phase with intention rather than loss.

Investment: Discussed during consultation

Seasonal Enrollment

Limited slots. Meaningful work. We open enrollment seasonally to protect the quality of each package. If you're drawn to one, reach out—let's see if we're aligned.
