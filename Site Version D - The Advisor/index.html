20+ Years Clinical Experience • 5,000+ Clinical Hours • PhD, LCSW
You've built a solid life — career, relationships, maybe a family — and yet there's a gap between how things look on the outside and how they feel on the inside.

That's not unusual. It's actually what brings most of my clients through the door.

What I offer isn't a diagnosis or a treatment plan. It's the space and the skill to understand what's really happening, and the support to move toward something better.

I've been doing this for nearly 20 years. That's 5,000+ clinical hours, advanced training in developmental psychology and trauma, and enough experience to know what actually works — and what doesn't. I work slowly, thoughtfully, and with genuine investment in your life. You're not a one-hour slot in my day. You're the reason I do this work.
