Investment & Fees

Transparent pricing

Individual Therapy

$200/hour individual | $300/hour couples

Sessions are typically 50 minutes. I provide superbills for out-of-network insurance reimbursement.

Why Private Pay

Staying private-pay is intentional. I'm not constrained by insurance protocols, session limits, or diagnoses. I can work at the pace your situation requires. The relationship is between us — not mediated by a third party.

It means I keep my practice small. Every person gets genuine investment and attention, not a slot in an overbooked schedule.

Wellness Packages

Package pricing varies by duration and frequency. Most range from $2,000 – $5,000. Payment plans available.

The Investment Conversation

I know therapy isn't free. And I know it's an investment to choose a private-pay provider. The people who work with me get clear value from it — they feel different, their relationships improve, their capacity to handle stress expands.

Worth isn't about the lowest price. It's about getting what you actually need.
