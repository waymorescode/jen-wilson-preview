Community

Building connection, fostering growth

Why Community Matters

Real change happens in connection — first in the therapy room, but also with other people working on similar questions.

That's why I host gatherings, workshops, and community events. These are warm, open spaces where you can think about something important with other people who care about similar things — conversations rooted in compassion and shared growth.

Upcoming Event

Inner Circle — Reinvention & Quiet Power

Thursday, July 10 · 6:00 – 7:30 PM · Seaside Spiritual Spa, St. Augustine

An intimate evening where women of all ages gather to reflect, reclaim, connect, and inspire one another. This curated, guided salon blends reinvention — the woman you're becoming — with quiet power, exploring gentle boundaries, self-respect, and choosing yourself without guilt.

The restorative conversation will leave you grounded, renewed, and more attuned to looking and feeling well from the inside out. The evening includes guided discussion and practical strategies for self-care and wellness to bring home.
