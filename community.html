Being Here, Not Just Practicing Here

I live in this community. My son attends school here. I buy groceries where you do. My roots are in the sand. I'm not a visiting expert—I'm a neighbor who happens to understand something about how to move through difficulty.

Because of that, I show up. I host gatherings. Workshops on what matters. Spaces where people can connect, learn, feel less alone in whatever they're navigating.
